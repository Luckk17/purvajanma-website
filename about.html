The Oracle

Who were you before this lifetime?

Purvajanma turns birth details into a shareable mythic identity: rank, realm, dominion, legacy, powers, and a full cinematic card styled around Indian historical imagination and Vedic symbolism.

It is designed as an entertainment-first experience. The goal is emotional resonance, beautiful presentation, and a result people want to save or share.

Vibe Dark, royal, ancient, cinematic. Gold fire, parchment, stars, temples, battlefields, courts, and legends.
Output A compact oracle card plus deeper scroll sections that explain your rank, era, powers, and myth.
Privacy The current static version runs in the browser. Birth details are used to generate the result on the page.
